Who We Are

Our Story & Values

Dedicated to curating experiences where fine dining, celebrations, and cinema achieve peak artistic expression.

A Vision of Elegance

Founded in Udaipur, Aurum Estate was born out of a desire to create a single premium location that solves multiple event, entertainment, and culinary desires for elite travelers and local residents alike.

Over the years, we have set a standard for quality, incorporating custom-tailored designs, master chefs, and advanced visual-audio technology into our assets to build unforgettable moments for our patrons.

Our Mission

To establish a premium luxury destination that delivers absolute customer satisfaction in fine dining, wedding events, and cinema viewing. We aim to execute every service to 5-star international hospitality standards.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier hospitality brand in Rajasthan, blending heritage Udaipur aesthetics with state-of-the-art technologies, elite dining, and high-capacity secure venue facilities.
